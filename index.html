About Me

With over 7.5+ years of hands-on experience in Mobile Application Development, specializing in React Native, I boast a robust track record of successful projects.

Throughout my career, I've remained dedicated to continuous learning, ensuring I stay abreast of the latest technologies.

While concurrently pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Applications, I am actively serving as an Application Engineer at Logically. Leveraging a diverse skill set that includes Java, Kotlin, Android Studio, Android Architecture Components, Android Jetpack, Material Design, Firebase, SQLite, Git, Google Play Services, React.js, Retrofit, OkHttp, JSON, and Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), I am dedicated to driving innovative solutions and contributing to organizational objectives.

Furthermore, I am enthusiastically interested in contributing to projects that involve cutting-edge technologies such as AI and Flutter app development.
